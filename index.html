I live in one of the richest counties in the country.

I feel a little startled when I think about living among so much wealth. I wonder, 'Do I earn my income meaningfully & sustainably? Do I spend my earnings meaningfully & sustainably?'.

I also feel ignorant. I find mysely trying to comprehend how much money really is around me. I'm not sure I can understand what it means that I'm in one of the the richest counties in America. What does that mean?
