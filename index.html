I live in one of the richest counties in the country.

I feel a little startled when I think about living among so much wealth. I wonder, 'Do I earn my income meaningfully & sustainably? Do I spend my earnings meaningfully & sustainably?'. I also feel ignorant. I find mysely trying to comprehend how much money really is around me.

What does income & poverty across Connecticut look like?

In this project, income levels and poverty levels are highlighted by town. Towns are outlined and color-coded shades of green based on average houshold income values (in the first chart) and color-coded shades of red based on the percent of household that live below poverty (in the second chart). The darker the green, the more income there is in that town. The darker the red, the higher the percentage of households live below the poverty level.*

* Income data is from census.gov
