Bezier Curves

quadraticCurveTo() is used against the CanvasRenderingContext2D obj.

This is used to render a quadractic curve to an existing path.

this requires a few params: cpx, cpy, x, y:

  • cpx: x-axis coord of control point
  • cpy y-axis coord of control point
  • x: x-axis coord of end point
  • y: y-axis coord of end point

Here, 3 points:

  • LIGHTBLUE: the "starting" point of the curve
  • DARKBLUE: the "ending" point of the curve
  • DARKGREEN: the bezier "control" point
